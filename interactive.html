🧪 States of Matter (drag the heat slider!)

Watch how particles behave in solids, liquids, and gases. More heat = more motion.

Solid ❄️

Tightly packed, vibrate in place. Fixed shape.

Liquid 💧

Close together, slide past each other. Takes container's shape.

Gas 💨

Far apart, fast in all directions. Fills the whole container.

🔥 Heat energy: 50°

🔑 Big idea: Mass stays the same in all three states — only particle spacing and motion change. When you heat matter, particles speed up and spread out (solid→liquid→gas).

🔑 Remember:
  • Closed loop + power + conductor = bulb lights up ✅
  • Insulators (rubber, wood, plastic) block current ❌
  • Any break in the loop = open circuit = no light ❌

🌙 The 8 Moon Phases

Click any phase to see what it looks like and when it appears. The full cycle takes ~29.5 days.

1. New Moon
No light visible
2. Waxing Crescent
Small sliver on right
3. First Quarter
Right half lit
4. Waxing Gibbous
Mostly lit on right
5. Full Moon
Fully lit
6. Waning Gibbous
Mostly lit on left
7. Last Quarter
Left half lit
8. Waning Crescent
Small sliver on left

🔑 Key vocabulary

Waxing = growing bigger (right side lights up first).
Waning = shrinking smaller (right side goes dark first).
Gibbous = more than half lit.
Crescent = less than half lit.
