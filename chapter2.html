🎂 Earth's Layers - Like a Giant Cake!

Our Earth is made of different layers, just like a delicious layered cake! Let's dig deep and see what's inside.

🏔️ CRUST

Where we live! Thinnest layer (5-70 km thick)

  • Mountains, oceans, continents
  • Made of solid rock
  • Cool temperatures

🔥 MANTLE

Largest layer! (2,900 km thick)

  • Hot, thick rock that flows
  • Temperature: 1,000-3,700°C
  • Creates volcanic activity

💧 OUTER CORE

Liquid metal layer (2,200 km thick)

  • Liquid iron and nickel
  • Temperature: 4,000-5,000°C
  • Creates Earth's magnetic field

⚪ INNER CORE

Solid metal center (1,220 km radius)

  • Solid iron and nickel
  • Temperature: Over 5,000°C!
  • Pressure is EXTREME!

💡 Did You Know?

If Earth were the size of an apple, the crust would be thinner than the apple's skin! The deepest hole ever dug by humans only went through a tiny bit of the crust - about 12 km deep!

🌍 Earth's Motion: Rotation & Revolution

🔄 ROTATION (Spinning)

Earth spins on its axis like a top!

⏱️ Time: 24 hours = 1 day

🌅 Causes: Day and night

↪️ Direction: West to East

🌍↻

Think about it: When you're facing the Sun, it's daytime. As Earth spins, you rotate away from the Sun into nighttime!

🌀 REVOLUTION (Orbiting)

Earth travels around the Sun in an oval path!

⏱️ Time: 365.25 days = 1 year

🍂 Causes: Seasons (summer, fall, winter, spring)

📍 Path: Ellipse (oval shape)

🌍 → ☀️

Fun Fact: That's why we have leap year every 4 years - to account for the extra 0.25 days!

💡 Remember This!

Easy way to remember:

  • ROTATION = Earth Rotates (spins) → Makes Day and Night
  • REVOLUTION = Earth Revolves (orbits) → Makes Years and Seasons

💡 Tip: Rotation is like a ballerina spinning, Revolution is like running around a track!

🌙 Moon Phases - Why Does the Moon Change Shape?

The Moon doesn't actually change shape! We just see different parts of it lit up by the Sun as it moves around Earth.

New Moon

Can't see it! Moon is between Earth and Sun

First Quarter

Half visible - right side lit up

Full Moon

Fully lit! Earth is between Sun and Moon

Last Quarter

Half visible - left side lit up

Complete cycle takes about 29.5 days!

💡 Remember This!

Easy way to remember moon phases:

  • WAXING = Getting bigger (like waxing a car adds layers!)
  • WANING = Getting smaller (waning means decreasing)
  • CRESCENT = Less than half visible (thin curved shape)
  • GIBBOUS = More than half visible (but not full)

🌙 Fun Memory Trick: "When the Moon is waxing, it's right-side shining. When it's waning, it's left-side dining!"
