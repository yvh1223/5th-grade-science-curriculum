💡 Test-Taking Tips

📖 Read Carefully:

Read each question twice before selecting your answer.

❌ Eliminate Wrong Answers:

Cross out answers you know are incorrect first.

⏱️ Manage Your Time:

Don't spend too long on one question. You can always come back.

✅ Review Your Answers:

Use any extra time to check your work before submitting.
